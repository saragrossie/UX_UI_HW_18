Hi, I’m Sara.

I am a UX/UI Researcher and Designer based in San Antonio, TX.



Before entering the world of UX/UI, I was an educator for many years. Coupled with my students eagerness to engage with learning, I attribute my success as a teacher to my ability to synthesize and deliver information in an accessible and digestible format that kept them engaged.

The process of understanding the student experience is what led me to UX/UI, as I believe understanding the user’s experience is paramount to achieving any end goal.

As a teacher and leader in education, I became highly skilled at resourceful problem-solving, giving and receiving feedback, and backwards planning from an end goal.

I am a problem solver by nature with developed skills in user research, information hierarchy, and project management.

I care deeply about delivering information that is accessible to any and all audiences.
